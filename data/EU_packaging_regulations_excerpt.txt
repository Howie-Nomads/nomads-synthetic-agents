EU Packaging and Waste Directive 2022 Excerpt

Article 6: Compostable packaging must meet EN 13432 standards.
Article 7: All packaging labeled "biodegradable" must specify degradation time and environmental conditions.
Article 10: Municipalities may define local collection processes, but coordination with waste processors is recommended.
Violations of labeling rules may result in fines between €5,000–€50,000 per SKU.
